# Finding: Authoritative Source Hierarchy

## Finding

Cosmos-Transfer decisions should prioritize official NVIDIA docs, local code, official GitHub, and Hugging Face model cards. Ecosystem claims should use NVIDIA Newsroom or company press releases. Research papers are evidence, but not operational guarantees.

## Evidence

- NVIDIA Docs and local code define actual public interfaces and constraints.
- Hugging Face model card defines model family, license, and user responsibility language.
- NVIDIA Newsroom and official company releases define adoption and ecosystem claims.
- Research pages and arXiv papers report evaluations, but those evaluations are scoped to their datasets and protocols.

## Impact

- Avoid treating partner adoption or research metrics as a blanket guarantee for Japanese dashcam training data.
- Keep implementation decisions anchored to A-grade sources.

## Confidence

High.

## Operational Recommendation

When a claim affects data trust or model selection, require A-grade evidence or explicitly label it as research/ecosystem context.
